What is this place?

Hello there! Welcome to the school etc. aggregator-linker site, an actually-intuitive collection of things related to school. Right now, we're focusing on collecting things like copies of notes, links to Quizlets, other files (like diagrams or PowerPoints), and similar items. Hopefully, Sealsite will be a cohesive place to more effectively access and share any relevant study material. At some point, we might branch out to new features (like a forum for asking questions or notifications when new content is added), but we're working to build a solid foundation first.
